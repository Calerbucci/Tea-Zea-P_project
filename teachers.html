What We Do

Finding Your Next Job

Your success is important to us. TeaZeaP has curated partnerships with various employers looking to fill full time, part time, and substitute teacher positions. Through our platform you will be promoted as a candidate for a number of openings. If your resume is a good match for our clients’ needs, a recruiter will reach out to you to ensure the opportunity fits your goals and preferences. From there, our team will prepare you for every step of the hiring process, from the interview to placement.

Apply

Apply by sending us your resume. A member of our team will then reach out to learn more about you, your skills, and desired work space.

Search

Our staff & clients work hand in hand to search and fill positions that match your desires and their needs.

Sign

Once the right position has been decided as the right fit contracts are negotiated and signed. Our team will continue to support you and maintain open communication throughout your contract.
